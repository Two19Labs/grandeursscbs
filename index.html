ABOUT GRANDEUR

Bridging Academics & Industry Realities

Founded at Shaheed Sukhdev College of Business Studies (SSCBS), Delhi University, Grandeur is a faculty-guided, student-driven body dedicated to mastering strategic consulting, operational design, financial evaluation, and sector research.

We empower our consultants to work directly with business founders and executive boards to provide data-driven insights. At the same time, we serve the student community by organizing workshops, publishing casebooks, and hosting case competitions.
